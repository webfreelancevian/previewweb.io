Our History

At Laspro, we offer a wide range of film equipment rentals in Jakarta. We know that filming is a lot more than just getting the shot—it’s about staying on budget, capturing the perfect angle, and making sure your equipment performs as advertised. That’s why we’ve worked hard to establish ourselves as one of the leading providers of professional film equipment rentals in Jakarta. We have everything you need for your next project: from cameras to lighting rigs to sound systems, we have it all!

We also offer a variety of services for those looking for expert advice on how to make the most out of their newly rented equipment. Whether you want help choosing the right camera or lighting rig for your project or advice on how best to use them once they’re in hand, we’re happy to help.
